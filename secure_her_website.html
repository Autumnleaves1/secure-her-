Secure Her

Cybersecurity Awareness for Women

Problem

Many women and young girls lack basic cybersecurity knowledge, making them vulnerable to online scams, harassment, and data theft.

Our Solution

A modern, mobile-friendly website that educates women about online safety through easy-to-understand content and interactive tools.

Steps We've Taken

  • Created learning modules and tips for beginners
  • Built a working prototype

Next Steps
